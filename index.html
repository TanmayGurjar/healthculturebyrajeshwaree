About Me

Hi, I'm Rajeshwareeba Jadeja, a Clinical Dietitian and Nutritionist based in Ahmedabad. I hold a Master’s degree in Food Science and Nutrition along with a Post-Graduate Diploma in Dietetics.

I founded Health Culture in 2020 with a mission to help people build sustainable and healthy eating habits using a scientific, evidence-based approach.

I specialize in weight management, diabetes care, PCOS, gut health, and therapeutic nutrition, focusing on long-term wellness.
